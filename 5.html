Ganesh Chaturthi 2021 date: When is Ganesh Chaturthi this year?

Ganesh Chaturthi 2021 Date: The annual festival of Ganesha Chaturthi marks the birth of Lord Ganesha, the son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati. It involves 10 days of celebration and takes place in the Bhadrapada month as per the Hindu calendar. The auspicious festival begins on september 10 this year, which is a Saturday. The festival inaugurates with people bringing or making clay idols (murtis) of the Lord, and concludes on the day of Anant Chaturdashi, when the idol is submerged in the nearest body of water, called Ganesha visarjan.

Lord Ganesha is one of the most worshipped deities amongst Indian Hindus. Also known as Vinayaka or Ganpati, the elephant God is revered as the God of beginnings and the remover of obstacles.
